Gather. Grow. Go.—Passionate about entering into full fellowship with God's Kingdom.

Mission

Overwhelmed by the gift of salvation, we have a heart to gather to worship as a church body being passionate about entering into full fellowship with God's Kingdom on a mission to see God's kingdom established across the earth.

Vision

  • Reintroducing the Kingdom of God on earth to mankind as result of the presence of the Spirit.
  • To restore righteousness and holiness to man.
  • To restore the Holy Spirit in Mankind.
  • To be retained in the behavior and mindset of the Kingdom so we can take charge of our circumstances rather than being a slave to them.
  • To restore the Kingdom rulership of God on Earth to mankind.

Theme & Strategy: Gather, Grow, Go

Gather

Focus on connecting through church and worship experiences where we develop relationships with Christ and with each other.

Grow

Learn more about God and his impact on our daily lives.

Go

Make disciples and being disciples in our community.

Core Beliefs

  • Biblical Expressions of worship include singing, clapping of hands, audible praise to God and shouting. (Psalm 150)
  • People are welcomed to attend in either casual clothes or in their "Sunday Dress" either way, you'll be welcomed.
  • That the church is the core of any community. We get to know each other both in and outside the four walls of the church.
  • That being a Christian should be both an inward and outward expression of God's love toward ourselves and each other.
  • We believe in showing love, grace, and mercy.

Core Beliefs continued

  • We believe the Bible is the inspired and only infallible written word of God, a revelation from God to man.
  • We believe that there is one God (Deut 6:4-9). He was the father in creation the son in redemption, and the Holy Ghost in the church (1 Tim 3:16).
  • We believe that salvation is by Faith in Jesus Christ and not by human works (Eph 2:8-9).
  • We believe that the ordinance of Baptism by a burial with Jesus Christ should be observed as Commonwealth in the scriptures.

Leadership

Bishop Harold B. Smith

Bishop Harold B. Smith has been Pastor of the Free Gospel Church, Inc. for 41 Years. He is a dedicated Shepherd who is focused on making disciples in the local church supporting the surrounding community. He is a preacher and teacher of God's Word. Pastor Smith loves the people of God and all are welcome when you visit or attend the Free Gospel Church of Culpeper. Bishop Smith married his bride, Margaret L. Smith, 58 years ago. Out of that union were 3 sons and 1 daughter.

Margaret L. Smith

Margaret L. Smith is the motherly example and sets the atmosphere at the church. She labored tirelessly for the Lord right beside her husband. She is devoted to praying for and with souls and her reputation for her spirit of hospitality will always be present.
